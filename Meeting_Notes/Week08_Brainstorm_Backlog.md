## Discussion Notes

### 1. Empathy Maps Review
All four empathy maps presented and reviewed:

| Stakeholder | Lead | Key Insights Identified |
|-------------|------|------------------------|
| Nurse Dispatcher | AA | Trust in AI is the critical adoption barrier — confidence score presentation is essential |
| Paramedic / Driver | AK | Pre-arrival clinical data significantly improves crew preparedness |
| AMI Patient / Caller | JO | Public awareness of new direct EMS pathway is a major gap |
| Dispatch Centre Manager | NM | Cultural gap between dispatch and ambulance must be addressed by system design |

**Cross-cutting insight agreed by team:** Post-callout feedback loops are the engine of AI improvement — must be designed in from Sprint 1, not added later.

### 2. Journey Maps Review
LH presented all four journey maps. Team agreed they accurately capture the end-to-end experience across four phases. Minor revision: AMI Patient journey Phase 1 updated to include the confusion between calling GP vs calling EMS directly.

### 3. POV Statements
NM led POV drafting session. Four POV statements agreed in format:
> [User] ... needs ... [need] ... because ... [insight]

All four reviewed and agreed. Recorded in Loop and in Section 3 of Part B report.

### 4. Ideation Brainstorm — HMW Questions
**Method:** Silent brainstorm (5 mins per stakeholder cluster on Loop whiteboard) → group sharing → dot-voting → prioritisation

**Results:**
- 48 raw ideas generated across 4 clusters
- 23 ideas shortlisted after dot-voting
- 16 mapped to product backlog items
- 8 HMW questions agreed per stakeholder (32 total)

**Sample HMWs by cluster:**
- Dispatcher: How might we design the AI panel so confidence scores build dispatcher trust?
- Paramedic: How might we ensure dispatch notifications include a minimum clinical data set?
- Patient: How might we coordinate with the Heart Foundation on direct EMS pathway awareness?
- Manager: How might we design a KPI dashboard that requires no IS support to read?

### 5. Backlog Planning
Team agreed on MoSCoW prioritisation for user stories. LH to facilitate Planning Poker session for story points next meeting. Target: 15 user stories, priority ordered.
