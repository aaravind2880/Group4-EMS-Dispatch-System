### 2. Project Understanding
Team reviewed NNSWLHD EMS case study. Key points:
- AMI is a leading cause of mortality (15% male, 11.7% female mortality in region)
- Current GP-intermediated pathway introduces ~2.5–3 hours cumulative delay before CRT commences
- Client requirements: direct citizen-to-EMS pathway + AI triage module + strict budget neutrality
- 9-week delivery timeline confirmed — tight but achievable with hybrid methodology

### 3. Methodology Decision
AA proposed hybrid approach. Team discussed four options: Agile, Waterfall, Scrum standalone, PRINCE2 standalone. Unanimous agreement on:
> **Hybrid: PRINCE2 + Scrum + Design Thinking (DT) + Rational Unified Process (RUP)**

Rationale: No single methodology addresses governance, iterative AI development, user-centred design, and system architecture simultaneously. The hybrid combines the strengths of each.
